About Us

We are a team of skilled and inspired individuals and our 'reason for being’(IKIGAI) is the film making process. Our sole purpose and the top most priority is client satisfaction and delivering the best. Brilliant ideas if not executed well, will just remain ideas; execution of ideas into reality is what excites us.

Our Aim

We aim at providing comprehensive film production services to our clients to ensure a seamless and smooth production experience for Movies/Advertisements in India.

Production Fixer India

IKIGAI is an India based company and our last project was “The Indian Detective” with Blue Eyes Pictures starring Russell Peters. We provide production fixer services in India to production houses and producers from around the world. We support our clients in shooting Documentaries, Docu-soaps, TV shows, TV Commercials, Digital Films, Music Videos and Feature Film projects in India. We facilitate Pre and Post production services including research on ideas, location scouting, legal permits, visa assistance, planning of logistics, scheduling, hiring of local crew and equipment’s.
